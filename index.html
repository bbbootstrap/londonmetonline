About London Metropolitan University

London Metropolitan University has a rich history, with strong academic roots that date back to 1848. Based in one of the world’s most exciting, dynamic and vibrant capital cities, London Met is home to a diverse community of inspiring and determined learners, teachers and innovative thinkers. We offer a wide range of courses, from undergraduate and postgraduate degrees to short courses and professional qualifications. In partnership with the ECA Group, a global leader in education in Australia, UK and internationally, London Metropolitan University delivers a world class Online MBA programme with a number of additional options that make it even more flexible and enriching for our students. These include extended student support across multiple continents, virtual internships, international study options, career guidance, and much more.

LONDON MET ONLINE Why Us?

London Met Online is a collaboration between the London Metropolitan University and the Education Center of Australia. Our coursework is delivered through ECA’s online division EOL and provides the same high quality education that students have come to expect from the London Met on-campus courses.

A Rich Tradition

London Met’s history dates back to 1848, when our founders established the London Metropolitan Classes for Young Men.We have built on our experience and today offer a variety of industry relevant courses using state-of-the-art technology.

End-to-End Student Support

Besides a continuously updated content base, we also offer our students with all the help they may need including application process, scheduling, career guidance.

Acclaimed Modern University

London Met has been named the best “millennial” institution in the UK in The Times Higher Education Young University Rankings.

A Highly Successful Alumni Base

London Met has produced a number of well-known industry leaders including Mayor of London, Sadiq Khan and Labour leader Jeremy Corbyn, comedian Vic Reeves, singer Alison Moyet and footballer Garth Crooks.

Career Progression and Placements

Whether you want to upskill yourself for your career progression or you want to enhance your skills to kick start your own business, London Met Online MBA Degree Program is your pathway to promotion, higher remuneration and job security.

Learn with the Best

Education Centre of Australia (ECA) with their corporate offices in Australia and India will be your delivery partner for London Met Online courses.

LONDON MET ONLINE Study How You Want, When You Want

At London Met Online, we believe each student is unique. Instead of forcing you to fit our courseware, we’ve designed a syllabus that fits around you, your preferences and lifestyle. London Met Online’s MBA is affordable, self-paced and offers access to the same quality curriculum as the on-campus MBA course.

With our weekend MBA programme, you can earn a world-class degree while working full-time. Expand your network, learn new skills, and take your management acumen to new heights, all while studying remotely.

No Exams, No Stress

We assess your performance based on how you learn and progress through the course instead of using exams which are unsuited to measuring a student’s potential in online settings..

Easy Admission Requirements

Your management experience counts, and you can sign up as long as you have a graduate degree/diploma*** with a minimum work experience of two years. London Met Online accepts graduate degrees and/or an advanced diploma including a professional qualification at Level six or above.

Flexible Course Structure & Schedule

Our course is tailored for working professionals and is available in small, interactive easy-to-study content modules. We offer self-paced study formats which let you learn at a pace best suited to you.
